About Us

Nepal Commerce Campus (NCC) participates in BIM Cricket Tournament in Nepal. Established in 2018, NCC is known for its competitive spirit and has consistently produced talented cricketers.

NCC has consistently been a strong contender in domestic tournaments, showcasing their skills and determination on the field.

The team is currently coached by Basanta Khatri and is captained by Manjeet Thapaliya.

Our Vision

To be a leading force in Nepalese cricket, inspiring and empowering young players within the community and beyond by maintaining the brotherhood. We strive to create a legacy of excellence, teamwork, and sportsmanship, while providing unforgettable cricketing experiences for our players, fans, and the entire nation.

Our Mission

To be the flag bearer of cricketing excellence within Nepal, inspiring and empowering young players through consistent hard work, dedication, and a strong team spirit. We aim to achieve sustained success in domestic cricket tournaments, representing NCC with pride and contributing to the growth of cricket in the nation.
